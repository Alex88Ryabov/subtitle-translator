# Privacy Policy — Course Subtitle Translator

_Last updated: July 5, 2026_

## What the extension does

Course Subtitle Translator translates English course subtitles into Ukrainian or
Russian and displays them over the video player.

## Data we process

- **Subtitle text.** To perform translation, the text of the course subtitles you
  are watching is sent to the translation service selected in the settings
  (Google Translate by default). No account identifiers, page URLs, or personal
  data are attached to these requests.
- **Local Chrome AI engine.** If you select the local engine, subtitle text is
  translated on your device and never leaves your browser.

## Data we store

- **Settings** (target language, display mode, engine, font size) are stored in
  your browser's extension storage (`chrome.storage.local`).
- **Translation cache.** Translated subtitle lines are cached locally so each
  lecture is translated only once. The cache never leaves your device.

## Data we do NOT collect

- No analytics, telemetry, or tracking of any kind.
- No personal information, browsing history, or account data is collected,
  stored, or transmitted to the developer.
- The developer operates no servers; the extension communicates only with the
  translation service you selected.

## Third-party services

When the Google Translate engine is selected, subtitle text is sent to
`translate.googleapis.com`. Google's own privacy policy applies to that
processing: https://policies.google.com/privacy
